Our Mission

Buildings only feel smart when they can sense what people feel. Our solution links a mesh of ESP32-powered sensors with a clean control layer so facility teams can keep air quality within healthy bounds, log historical context, and automate ventilation or climate scenes with the touch of a button.

From Lab to Living Spaces

Every device we deploy is calibrated inside TU Eindhoven labs, stress-tested across dorms, lecture halls, and collaborative studios, and then handed over with APIs that connect directly to your smart hub of choice. Whether you automate windows, fans, or lighting, our data stream translates into actionable routines.

  • Edge processing on ESP32 ensures instantaneous alerts.
  • Secure gateway shares metrics with building management systems.
  • Open integration hooks tie into Home Assistant, KNX, or custom apps.
